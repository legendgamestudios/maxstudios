Introduction.

Founded in 2023 by Vex, and Swar, Legend Game Studios was introduced into the world of roblox studio creators. They then went on to hire someone named Ash, as the 3rd founder, because him and Swar we're best friends. Together as 3, they then worked tirelessly working on their first project; Drift Masters.

Now, Drift Masters wasn't your ordinary racing game. They designed it to produce UI flexibility, and performance. And, Man did they succeed, Drift Masters introduced new players to the LGS (abbreviation for Legend Game Studios) community. They then gathered members to join their community server on the platform Discord.

With these new players now in their Discord Community, they could now start hiring game developers and staff. Vex decided to buy robux, and buy a gamepass from the group in order to be able to pay their new staff. Since then they are still trying to grow LGS into a great community. To join the team along our journey, you can join our Discord. We hope to see you there!

Our Projects.

Our main project is Drift Masters. To get announcement notifications when we release a new game, you can join our Discord.
